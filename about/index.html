ABOUT & CONTACT

Kwame began experimenting with computer programs as a young adult before attending community college in 2003 for a courses in Computer Science.

His practice continued in 2011, offering my services to clients. The digital art designs created have been utilized for print-on-demand items, clothing, digital art, business cards, and CDs.

Kwame Deswan's professional experience has a strong foundation in Audio Production and Engineering. As an emerging DJ, at the age of 17, Kwame was hired for Dancehall and Soca music at nightclubs and private parties in Albany, NY.

After DJing, Kwame became the sole operator of a recording studio in Albany, NY, engineering studio quality music for local Hip Hop and R&B artists.

In 2013, Kwame relocated to Austin, TX. He grew an interest in Network Security and in 2016 started working as a Systems Administrator for MindShift, a Ricoh Company.

In 2022, Kwame started a position as a Software Support representative at LogicMonitor, a hybrid observability software company.

Currently, Kwame is focused on strengthening his knowledge in front and backend development.
