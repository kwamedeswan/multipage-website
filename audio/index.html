Why is audio engineering important?

An audio engineer helps to produce a recording or a live performance, balancing and adjusting sound sources using equalization, dynamics processing and audio effects, mixing, reproduction, and reinforcement of sound.

Sound engineers don’t necessarily just work in music but moreso sound. Audio is used everywhere today in digital content. High quality audio makes all the difference in the viewers experience. Blog videos, or audio within paid ads, need quality audio to be effective.
